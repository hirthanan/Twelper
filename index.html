What is Twelper?

A mobile app for planning out your vacation.

Toronto

Toronto, a dynamic metropolis with a core of soaring skyscrapers, with the iconic CN Tower.

San Francisco

San Francisco, a city known for its, iconic Golden Gate Bridge, cable cars and colorful Victorian houses.

New York

New York City comprises 5 boroughs sitting where the Hudson River meets the Atlantic Ocean.

Seattle

Seattle, home to a large tech industry, with Microsoft and Amazon headquartered in its metropolitan area.

London

London, capital of England and the United Kingdom, imposing the iconic Big Ben, and the London Eye.

How does it work?

First, you enter your location and the number of attractions you'd like to visit. Then, Twelper will retrieve the highest rated attractions using the Yelp API, and then map out the least costly route using the Uber API.
